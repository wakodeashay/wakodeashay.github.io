Ashay Wakode

I am a System Engineer at Autonomous Robotics Research Centre, Technology Innovation Institue in Abu Dhabi. I work on multi-agent planning and guidance of fixed-wing UAVs.

I graduated from Indian Institue Of Technology Bombay, India with B.Tech and M.tech in Mechanical Engineering with a research award for my Master thesis. During my master's research, I developed a novel inverse kinematics formulation for generating manipulator trajectories that avoid singularities under the guidance of Prof. Anirban Guha from Mechanical Engineering Department.

As a part of my Undergraduate research, I focused on Online Coverage Path Planning problem, advised by Arpita Sinha from System and Controls Department. Here, I explored the usefulness of Space-Filling Curves for coverage in unstructured environments.

To explore my research interest in multi-agent planning and control, I am working towards developing multi-agent patrol strategies for agents with limited battery capacity, patrolling workspaces with priority locations and recharge stations.
