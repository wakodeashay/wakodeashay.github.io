Ashay Wakode

I am a System Engineer at Autonomous Robotics Research Centre, Technology Innovation Institue in Abu Dhabi, working on Cooperative Control and Guidance of Fixed-wing UAVs.

I graduated from Indian Institue Of Technology Bombay, India with a Dual degree (B.Tech + M.tech) in Mechanical Engineering with a Research award for Master thesis. During which, I developed a novel Inverse Kinematics formulation for generating manipulator trajectories that avoid singularities under the guidance of Prof. Anirban Guha.

As a part of my Undergrad research, I focused on Online Coverage Path Planning problem. Here, I showed the usefulness of Space-Filling Curves for coverage in unstructured environments.

Currently, I am working towards developing Multi-agent Patrol strategies for agents with limited battery capacity patrolling workspaces with priority locations.
